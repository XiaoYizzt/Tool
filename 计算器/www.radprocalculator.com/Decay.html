Radioactive Decay   
Useful for calculating today's activity for any radioactive isotope. You may also back decay sources to find out the original activity (or for any date), knowing the current activity. If the isotope that you wish to decay is not on the drop down list, check the 'not listed' check-box and manually enter the isotope name and its half-life to perform the calculation. You may enter date or time or a combination of both. Calendar pickers are included for date entry convenience.
