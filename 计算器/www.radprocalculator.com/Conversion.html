becquerels curies Activity Conversions
Select Conversion   Converted Units = Input Units Enter Value         
Converts all common units of radioactivity measurement including activity units, dose equivalent units and absorbed dose units.  Also included are other common multi-unit calculations such as uCi/ml to pCi/L and dpm/100cm2 to Bq/cm2 (miscellaneous option button).  The calculator includes both SI and customary US radiation units.
